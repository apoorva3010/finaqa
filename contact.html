Requirements in order to empanel with FinaQA®

As mentioned in the application and the website, FinaQA® is interested in taking services of the following:

SEBI Registered Investment Advisors like

  • CFA - Certified Financial Analyst

  • NISM Certificate holders - Series X- A Investment Advisor Level 1 & Series X- B Investment Advisor Level 2
